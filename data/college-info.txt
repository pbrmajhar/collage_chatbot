About the School

The school specializes in training students to become IT engineers through a highly practical ("超実践") curriculum. Instead of focusing only on theory, students learn by building real projects, participating in team development, and practicing workflows similar to those used in software companies. The curriculum was developed with support from over 100 IT companies.
